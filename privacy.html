Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Curata TrainIQ ("we," "our," or "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

How the App Works

When you use Curata TrainIQ:

  • You input your workout preferences (duration, target muscles, workout style)
  • This information is sent to OpenRouter, our AI processing service
  • OpenRouter processes your inputs to generate a personalized workout
  • The generated workout is returned to your device and displayed to you
  • We do not store your workout preferences or generated routines

Information We Process

Workout Input Data

  • Workout duration preferences
  • Target muscle group selections
  • Workout style preferences
  • Any additional workout parameters you specify

Important: This data is sent to OpenRouter for AI processing but is not stored by us or OpenRouter after the workout is generated.

Information We Do NOT Collect or Store

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Workout history or exercise logs
  • Health or fitness tracking data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or tracking information
  • User accounts or profiles

Third-Party Service Provider

OpenRouter

We use OpenRouter as our AI processing service to generate your personalized workouts. When you request a workout:

  • Your workout preferences are sent to OpenRouter's servers
  • OpenRouter processes this information to generate your workout
  • The generated workout is returned to your device
  • OpenRouter does not retain your data after processing

OpenRouter acts as a data processor on our behalf and is bound by their own privacy policy. You can review OpenRouter's privacy practices at their website.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information during transmission:

  • All data sent to OpenRouter is encrypted in transit
  • We use secure HTTPS connections
  • No workout data is stored on our servers
  • OpenRouter processes data ephemerally (temporarily) without retention

Data Retention

We do not store your workout preferences or generated routines. OpenRouter processes your requests ephemerally, meaning your data is processed temporarily and not retained after your workout is generated.

Your Privacy Rights

Since we don't store your personal information, there's no persistent data for us to access, correct, or delete. However, you have the right to:

  • Know what information is being processed (as outlined in this policy)
  • Stop using the service at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

App Store Information

  • App download and update information
  • Crash reports (if you choose to share them)
  • App store reviews and ratings

This information is governed by your app store's privacy policy, not ours.
